My Project(IA)

By Brian Enchill 11301355

Assigned by our Web Design Lecturer for an Interim Assessment, we undertook the task of replicating the University of Ghana Legon's Computer Science Department website. Each student was responsible for creating and populating 10 pages, merging theoretical knowledge with practical web design skills. The objective was to showcase creativity, technical proficiency, and understanding of user experience by emphasizing attention to detail, design principles, and accurate replication of the existing online platform. The project aimed not only to meet requirements but also to contribute positively to the department's digital representation, demonstrating professionalism and application of web design concepts.
